Our Story

Exoticus was founded in 2018 by a team of horticultural specialists, wellness advocates, and scientists who shared a vision: to elevate cannabis cultivation to an art form while prioritizing therapeutic benefits and sustainable practices.

Our journey began with a simple observation‚Äîthe cannabis market was divided between medical applications that lacked artistry and recreational products that overlooked nuance. We set out to bridge this gap, creating products that honor both the science and soul of the plant.

Today, our team continues to innovate while staying true to our core principles: purity, precision, and a deep respect for the natural world that provides these remarkable botanicals.

Our Brand Philosophy

At Exoticus, we believe that cannabis, when cultivated with intention and respect, can be a powerful tool for wellness and personal exploration.

Our approach combines time-honored horticultural techniques with cutting-edge science, resulting in products of exceptional quality and consistency.

We are committed to transparency, sustainability, and education‚Äîempowering our community to make informed choices about their wellbeing.

OMMA Compliance & Lab Testing

Exoticus operates in full compliance with all Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority regulations. Our products are:

  • Lab tested for potency and purity
  • Packaged in child-resistant containers
  • Labeled with comprehensive usage information
  • Tracked through the state's inventory system

Lab results are available for every batch upon request.
